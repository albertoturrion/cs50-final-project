Why English Inmersion?

When I started to learn English, I felt I wasn’t moving forward. After making some test by myself, I realized I should learn only in English, avoiding the translation from English to Spanish - my native language - or vice-versa.

Because of this, I decided to make this app to help other people in the same situation, providing some resources and techniques which have been extremely useful for me.

You should learn English in English, although sometimes it will help you to understand the word in Spanish and then, translate it to English. However, as the aim is thinking in English, I urge you to do all possible in English:

  • Search in Google in English.
  • Write daily messages in English.
  • Watch series in English (you can activate subtitles in English).

Keep your motivation

At the start of learning English, probably you will realize that you are moving forward fast.

On the other hand, it’s likely you get frustrated while learning English if you have an intermediate level, so you should look for strong motivations and remember them to yourself in the bad moments.

Make a list of reasons that motivate you to learn, for instance:

  • Being promoted in your job.
  • Travelling around the world.
  • Being able to do courses in English with full comprehension.

You should make your own list. In any case, it is important to remember the following sentence:

When motivation fails you, discipline takes over.
